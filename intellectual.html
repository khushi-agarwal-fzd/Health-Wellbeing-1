INTELLACTUAL HEALTH

What do you mean by intellactual?

Intellectual means involving a person's ability to think and to understand ideas and information. High levels of lead could damage the intellectual development of children.The adjective intellectual describes something related to or using the mind or intellect. Your creative pursuit of singing in a rock band is different from your intellectual interest in 16th-century drama.

Signs that your intellactual health is good:-

  • You enjoy reading and keeping up with current events.
  • You do not hesitate from exposing yourself to new ideas and beliefs.
  • You are comfortable meeting new people.
  • You do not feel uncomfortable about changes in your routine.
  • You are a critical thinker.

How is intellactual health connected?

Being in a good mental state can keep you healthy and help prevent serious health conditions. A study found that positive psychological well-being can reduce the risks of heart attacks and strokes. On the other hand, poor mental health can lead to poor physical health or harmful behaviors.
